FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

I'm interested in receiving care from Navigation Medical Center. Where do I start?

Thank you for considering us for your care! You'll need to register with us before making your visit. See our new patient page for more details.

How do I see my doctor? Do I need to make an appointment?

We have a preference for appointments. You can schedule an appointment by calling (713) 223-4466. We will provide an appointment date within the next 24 hours. If a traditional office visit does not suit your schedule, telemedicine is also available.

We do offer walk-ins. We are able to meet with approximately 10 walk-in patients in the morning and afternoon. However, note that this is dependent on availability. To ensure that you receive care, we recommend scheduling a visit with us beforehand.

Will I get a reminder or confirmation for my appointment?

We will make a courtesy call to you 12-24 hours in advance of your appointment time as a reminder and confirmation. We do ask, however, that you assume all responsibility for remembering your reservation. If you do not show for your appointment, you may be responsible for a no-show fee. Refer to our cancellation policy for details.
