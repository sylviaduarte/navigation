NEW PATIENT INFORMATION

Thank you for choosing Navigation Medical Center as your primary care provider!

We are looking forward to providing the best possible comprehensive health care for you and your family.

There are a couple of things that we ask of you to do before your first visit with us. If you have any questions about this information, please call us at (713) 223-4466.

Registration

As a first-time patient, you will need to fill out our registration form. The registration process should take no more than 15 minutes. We encourage you to register before your visit with us; our office can enter your information into our system and verify your insurance benefits beforehand which will help minimize any possible wait time.

You can register with us electronically. You can also print out our form from home. In that case, please bring your form completely filled out or fax it to (281) 268-7908.

We do not accept registration via e-mail.

How should you prepare for your doctor's visit?

To ensure smooth scheduling, we ask you that arrive to our office 15 minutes before your scheduled appointment.

Please bring the following for your initial and upcoming appointments:

  • Insurance information
  • Identification
  • List of current medication(s)
  • Registration form (for new patients registering on paper)
  • Parent, guardian, or authorized person (for patients under 18)

Need to reschedule or cancel?

In order to provide you and others with the best care possible, we ask that you make every effort to keep your scheduled appointments and arrive in a timely manner.

If you are unable to make it to your scheduled appointment, please let us know by calling our office within 24 hours. Otherwise, a $15 no-show fee may apply.
